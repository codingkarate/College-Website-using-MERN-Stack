ABOUT IDEAL INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING

The Ideal Institute of Engineering (IIE) stands as a testament to the visionary pursuit of The Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, conceived to establish a center of excellence in technical education. Founded in 2010, the institute received AICTE approval and affiliation from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), officially embarking on its academic journey in 2011.
Nestled in the vibrant city of Kalyani, IIE enjoys a strategic and accessible location, directly opposite Kalyani Silpanchal Railway Station. More than just an institution, IIE is a catalyst for innovation and transformation, committed to nurturing future engineers with a dynamic blend of academic brilliance, industry-oriented learning, and ethical leadership.
